Your Associate Degree is the Perfect Stepping Stone

You know the BEST thing about being a graduate of our Associate of Arts in Liberal Arts program? Not only have you been prepared with the professional training you need in the workforce, but it’s also the perfect stepping stone toward a bachelor’s degree—and even more career opportunities.

Three Reasons Why a Bachelor’s Is Worth It

Expect a Higher Than Average Salary

Getting a bachelor’s is a good move for your wallet, especially in Texas. On average, bachelor’s graduates in Texas earn more than $52k annually—that’s higher than the national average for bachelor’s degree holders.*

The more education you have, the more jobs you’ll qualify for. In fact, 30% of jobs today require a bachelor’s degree,* and that number is projected to grow another 10% by 2026.**
