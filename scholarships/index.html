The Easy Way to Pay for Your HT Degree

Scholarships are a smart way to pay for your HT degree; the applications are usually free, the money never has to be paid back, and both undergraduate and graduate students qualify. But there’s no need to go scholarship hunting—we’ve already done that for you! Explore this page to find the top scholarships for adult HT students:

HT Scholarship

If you’re already an HT student and have a GPA of at least 2.5, you’re eligible for the HT Scholarship! Just submit a 500-word essay related to this year’s topic along with your application.

Apply Now

Local Scholarships

There are several scholarships just for Texas residents and students of Texas universities. Depending on the scholarship, you’ll be eligible based on factors such as age, race, academic performance, financial need, and what subject you’re studying:

  • Beacon Scholarship for Rural America
  • CBC Spouses Education Scholarship
  • Rebekah Assembly of Texas Irma Gesche Scholarship
  • Stars Scholarship Fund
  • Texas Urban Scholarship Fund

National Scholarships

No matter where you’re living in the U.S., there are tons of national scholarships available to cover education costs. We’ve even included some that are specifically made for working adults and parents. Depending on the scholarship, you’ll be eligible based on factors such as age, race, academic performance, financial need, and what subject you’re studying:

  • Adult Skills Education Program (ASEP) Scholarship
  • Adult Students in Scholastic Transition
  • Jeannette Rankin Women’s Scholarship Fund
  • Return2College Scholarship
  • Working Parent College Scholarship

We know this is a lot, but you don’t have to figure it out alone. Our enrollment coaches will help you find scholarships that work for you, making your HT degree that much more affordable: 512.505.6455.
