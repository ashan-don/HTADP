Influence the Law in Your Community

As a student of Huston-Tillotson University’s Bachelor of Arts in Criminal Justice, you’ll gain an in-depth perspective on law enforcement, courts and corrections, and local and federal law procedures. You’ll graduate ready to increase your impact in your community as well as your career prospects in the criminal justice discipline.

Gain Valuable Insights You Need to Protect Your Community

The criminal justice courses are focused on providing you with skills that will prepare you for real-world opportunities within the criminal justice discipline, including police work, criminology, forensics, and more. This degree will prepare you to:

Understand the procedures of law enforcement, court systems, and corrections at the city, county, state, and federal level.

Analyze real-world situations where law enforcement plays a role, including crime prevention, crime scene reconstruction, evidence gathering, probation and parole, and more.

Comprehend how the criminal justice system has evolved over time and how its history has influenced the current state of the system.

Experience Career-Focused Curriculum

As a criminal justice student, you’ll learn from professors with years of experience in law, criminology, and police work, helping you gain up-to-date, in-demand skills you need in the real world.

Courses you’ll take:

  • American Court System
  • Blacks in Criminal Justice
  • Community Policing
  • Corrections and Rights
  • Crime Prevention
  • Criminal Investigation
  • Criminal Law and Procedures
  • Criminology
  • Introduction to Criminal Justice
  • Juvenile Justice
  • Methods in Social Research
  • Police Practices and Procedures
  • Probation and Parole
  • Professional Development
