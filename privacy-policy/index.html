Privacy Policy

This web site is owned and operated by Helix Education, Inc. (“Helix Education”). Helix Education created and operates this web site. Helix Education is committed to respecting online privacy and recognizes your need for appropriate and reasonable protection and management of the personal information you share with us on this web site. As a reflection of that commitment, this Privacy Policy describes how Helix Education collects and uses any personally identifiable information that you submit via this web site, as well as other information that Helix Education may collect and use.

Information Collected

When you submit forms on our web site, we collect certain identifying information about you, which may include your name, street address, telephone number, e-mail address, or other identifying information. Except as otherwise explained below, this information is stored on our servers and used to fulfill your information request. Wherever your information is maintained at Helix Education, we intend to protect the information you provide us from unauthorized access or disclosure by, among other things, using firewalls.

Helix Education may provide this identifying information to third-parties, such as, without limitation, call centers, to fulfill your information request. Helix Education does not intend to otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, unless such transfer is legally required in order to comply with the law or respond to lawful requests or legal process.

Choice/Opt-Out

You may choose whether to supply us with personal information. Every email we send out also includes a link to change any personally identifiable information previously provided, change your communication preferences, or to opt out of our further use of such information. To request changes to any of your personally identifiable information, you may also email us at discovery@helixeducation.com.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our web site may use cookies (small files on your computer), to collect information about web site usage and to understand where visitors link to or otherwise use our web site. We also may use cookies to simplify the re-entering of your data, and to show you ads on sites across the Internet. Our use of Cookies helps us to improve our web site and your use of the web site. Many browsers allow you to control cookies, including whether or not to accept them and how to remove them.

Tracking technologies may record information such as Internet domain names and host names, Internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser software, operating system types, and dates and times our web site is accessed. We keep this information for an indefinite amount of time to improve the operation of our site and to provide better services to our users.

Contacting You

Helix Education may utilize different methods of responding to information requests, such as mailing brochures or replying via e-mail or telephone. Unless you otherwise opt out as identified above, by using our service to request information from Helix Education, you consent to Helix Education (or our partners and vendors) to contact you using the methods of their choice, to fulfill your information request, even if you may be listed on the National Do-Not-Call Registry.

Unless you otherwise opt out as identified above, Helix Education reserves the right to contact you in the future to help improve our operations or to introduce new services, such as surveys, to ensure that we have followed up with your information requests in a timely manner.

Children’s Policy

Helix Education web sites are not directed at individuals under thirteen (13) years of age. We do not intend to collect personal information from such individuals. If we learn that we have collected personal information from any individuals under the age of thirteen without parental consent, we will delete that information as soon as possible. If you believe that we may have collected personal information from an individual under the age of thirteen, please contact us at privacyconcerns@helixeducation.com.

If any modification is unacceptable to you, your only recourse is to terminate your use of this web site.
