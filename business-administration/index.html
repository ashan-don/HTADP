Our Business Administration courses are specifically built with the career-focused curriculum you need to compete in the job market and confidently lead in businesses big and small. This degree will prepare you to:

A Fast Track to Graduation

Business Administration classes are offered on-campus, conveniently located in downtown Austin, just one night a week. Plus, classes only last 5–6 weeks each, so you can work a full-time job while in school and still finish fast.
